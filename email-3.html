Slash Energy Costs with ThermaBoost Heat Reclaim
 
CO2 ThermaBoost integrates into your Hillphoenix system to leverage excess heat generated from your refrigeration system for comfort heating in stores of all sizes.
 
  • Up to 6 times more efficient than electric resistance heating
  • Delivers up to 3 times more heat output than traditional coils
  • Dramatically reduces or eliminates fossil-fuel-based auxiliary heating
 
Discover ThermaBoost
 
Maximize Efficiency with Low-Pressure Ejectors
 
Ideal for small to medium stores, low-pressure ejectors increase cooling capacity and reduce compressor workload by capturing and reusing energy from your CO2 system.
 
  • Lowers operating costs, with peak savings of 15-20%
  • Boosts energy efficiency, especially in warm climates
  • Improves reliability and reduces maintenance
 
Discover Low-Pressure Ejectors
 
Optimize Performance with Parallel Compression
 
Ideal for medium to large stores, Hillphoenix parallel compression technology leverages a dedicated flash-gas compressor to enhance efficiency and boost capacity.
 
  • Slashes average energy costs by up to 10%
  • Reduces compressor workload, extending compressor lifespan
  • Boosts overall capacity and minimizes energy waste
